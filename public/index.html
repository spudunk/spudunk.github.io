About Me

My name is Christopher Hicks. I am a web developer. I like to build things that are elegant, simple, and fast.

My main focus is the user experience and function of a landing page. I build lean, clean webpages that strongly prioritize SEO, fast page loads, and quick communication.

My goal is to build 100 websites for small businesses by the end of 2022. With the way the world and our economies are going, I recognize that it's time to expand my brand, and make the most of the time I have to build now because I have people counting on me.

Pricing

Landing Page - $200

I build websites starting at $200. Shop around and you'll realize that is a great deal! This is my expertise and how I plan to change the world.

This is for a basic page like you see here. I provide a worksheet. You provide branding, content, and choose a basic theme. It will pass all applicable Lighthouse tests and look great on any modern device.

The entire process takes about a week on my end. It may take longer to get the content together and make design decisions if this isn't done.

Branding Consult - $200

To build a website, I need your branding guide. Without a mission statement, slogan, logo, color scheme, and content, there isn't much I can do.

My expertise isn't branding, but I've done my research and put together a process for making a "good enough" branding guide to make an effective landing page.

Web Management - $50 / month

Don't let your website expire and get snatched up by a reseller, and don't let your content get stale!

I'll keep your website online, provide monthly traffic statistics, weekly updates, and make sure your hosting and domain renewals happen on time.

Bigger Projects - $40 / hour

I'll admit, I'm not the best web developer for complex multi page apps with lots of functionality yet. My goal is to help small companies get online, not take online companies to the next level.

I'm happy to discuss larger projects, and if I can't tackle them I will refer you to a designer who is great at what you want.
