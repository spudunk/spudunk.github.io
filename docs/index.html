About Me

My name is Christopher Hicks. I like to build things that are simple, elegant, and fast.

I've taken my hobby of coding and turned it into my occupation because I see a big gap between the small businesses I love and the technologies big companies use.

My goal is to build 100 websites for small businesses by the end of 2022.

My Work

For most people these days, their phones are the primary way of finding goods and services. I build lean, clean mobile first websites that prioritize SEO, fast page loads, and user experience.

This website characterizes my builds. I don't put anything in the way of the content. Everything loads fast, even on slow connections. It's simple, to the point, provides visitors the information they are looking for, and provides me with market insight.
